Our Purpose

Save An Aggie is an application made for Aggie by Aggies that will give NCAT students an outlet to ask for help academically, circumstantially, or physically. On the other hand, this application will allow Aggies to help satisfy requests made by their fellow peers for mutual benefit. Our main purpose other than giving an outreach reasource for Aggie students, is to allow peers to meet and collaborate to make the the North Carolina A&T campus a second home for all students.

How It Works

Register

Register and login with your NCAT email through the Save An Aggie Login portal.

Submit A Task

Once register and logged in, navigate to the request form to submit a task.

Help An Aggie

If you wish to help a fellow aggie, navigate to the task protal to select and take on a task.
